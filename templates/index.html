Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers.

Is this platform free to use?

Yes! FreelanceHub is completely free for both clients and freelancers. There are no hidden fees or subscription charges. We offer transparent, straightforward platform access.

How does the mock payment system work?

Payments are simulated within the system. When you confirm payment for completed work, it's marked as paid in the system. This allows you to experience the full project workflow safely in a demo environment.

How do freelancers receive their payments?

Once you approve the freelancer's work and complete the mock payment, the payment status updates in the system. In a production environment, payments would be transferred to the freelancer's account.

What happens if there's a dispute?

Our support team can help mediate disputes between clients and freelancers. We encourage communication through the platform's chat system. In case of conflicts, we work towards a fair resolution.

How do I know freelancers are verified?

All freelancers on our platform undergo verification checks. You can view their profiles, ratings, completed projects, and client reviews. Verified users have a checkmark badge on their profile.

Is my data secure on this platform?

This is a demonstration platform built for learning purposes. It uses secure practices but should not be used for production scenarios. For production use, implement proper security measures, SSL encryption, and comply with data protection regulations.

Can I cancel a project after posting?

Yes, you can cancel an active project. If freelancers have already submitted proposals, you should communicate with them before cancellation. Be professional and considerate in your interactions.

What's the commission structure?

FreelanceHub operates commission-free for both clients and freelancers during this demo period. In the future, a transparent commission model may be introduced with clear terms.
